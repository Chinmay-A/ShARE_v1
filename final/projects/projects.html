Learning Unit Projects




ShARE IIT Bombay stood 2nd in the Asia Case Study Challenge organized by ShARE. 15 teams from Asia competed for the best learning unit or group presentations of the ShARE Leadership programme and the top 3 slide decks were presented to a ShARE project lead.

The City and Consumer learning unit comprised Anuj Agrawal, Sunandinee Mehra, Kartik Laddha, and Shreyasi Mandal, and worked on the topic "How will sustainable and inclusive cities in your country look like in 2030?". Learning Unit Manager Rohith Karthikeyan and Senior Member Bhavya Jain spearheaded the project.
